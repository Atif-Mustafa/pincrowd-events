About Pincrowd Events

Crafting extraordinary experiences since 2020

Our Journey

Pincrowd Events is a trailblazing event management company nestled in the vibrant suburbs of Mumbai. Established in 2020, we stand out for delivering bespoke, high-quality experiences across diverse industries like Pharma, FMCG, and Finance. With years of experience and a client-first mindset, Pincrowd offers end-to-end event management globally.

Our Mission

Execution of high-quality, technology-driven events that create engaging experiences and build long-term client trust. Focus on client satisfaction and deliver 100% return on investment.

Our Vision

Providing out-of-the-box innovative solutions for clients, curating exhilarating and exclusive experiences with the highest standards of quality.

Our Services

Event Management

Virtual/physical events, brand/product launches, exhibitions, corporate events, award ceremonies, business meets, concerts, sports events, and more.

MICE & Logistics

End-to-end travel and logistics for multi-location events. We manage transportation, accommodation, and on-site coordination across global destinations.

Talent Management

From emcees to dance troops, singers, and performers. We connect you with top-tier talent to elevate your event experience.
